reflection – Adam Tervort http://adamtervort.com/ Principles for Smarter Knowledge Work Tue, 11 Aug 2020 03:11:59 +0000 en-US hourly 1 https://wordpress.org/?v=5.5 https://adamtervort.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/08/cropped-android-chrome-512x512-1-32x32.png reflection – Adam Tervort http://adamtervort.com/ 32 32 Balance https://adamtervort.com/2020/02/balance/ Fri, 14 Feb 2020 03:10:00 +0000 https://adamtervort.com/?p=2578 I have a pen that is great to write with when the cap is on the back. Take the cap off, however, and the balance is off. Writing with it is a chore because it doesn’t feel right. I want my pen to write when I tell it to, and an unbalanced pen doesn’t do that very well.

Life can get out of balance too. Sometimes imbalance happens to us, and sometimes we do it to ourselves. The quickest way to knock my life out of balance is to install Netflix on my phone. If it’s there I end up watching it. Once I’m in the app it’s easy to find a show that I like, and even easier to justify watching just one episode. My life is then out of balance! (I’m such a sucker for binge watching good TV shows. I can never watch just one episode.)

Learn what puts you out of balance and then make a plan to avoid those things.
